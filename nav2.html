A "Silent Disco Dandiya" is a unique and modern twist on traditional Dandiya Raas, a popular folk dance from the Indian state of Gujarat. In a traditional Dandiya event, participants dance to the rhythm of live music created by instruments like the dhol (drum) and the melodious tunes of folk songs. However, a "Silent Disco Dandiya" adds a contemporary and fun element to this cultural dance form.

A Flash Mob Dandiya is an impromptu gathering where people, often in public places, suddenly start dancing the traditional Indian folk dance Dandiya Raas. Participants, often strangers, converge and perform coordinated dance moves to lively music, surprising onlookers. These flash mobs celebrate cultural unity and bring an element of spontaneity to traditional festivities.
