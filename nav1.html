DAY-1 CELEBRATION

AS ON OCTOBER 15,2023

"Let the festivities commence, as we embark on the joyous journey of Navratri, seeking the blessings of Goddess Durga to fill our lives with happiness and prosperity! Jai Maa Durga!"

"On this auspicious first day of Navratri, we invoke the divine blessings of Goddess Shailputri. She represents purity and strength. As we light the lamp of devotion, may her grace illuminate our lives with joy, prosperity, and success. Jai Maa Shailputri!"

Navratri, a nine-night Hindu festival, begins with great fervor and devotion on its first day. This auspicious occasion marks the commencement of the annual worship of Goddess Durga, the divine feminine force symbolizing strength and purity. Navratri is celebrated with vibrant colors, traditional dances, and devotional fervor across India and among Hindu communities worldwide.
